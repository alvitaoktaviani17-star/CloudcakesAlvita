Cloudcakes Bakery

is a charming bakery nestled in the heart of the city, known for its delightful array of freshly baked goods and cozy atmosphere. Established in 2025, Cloudcakes has become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike, offering everything from artisanal breads to decadent pastries. The bakery prides itself on using high-quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create treats that not only taste amazing but also bring a sense of comfort and joy to every bite. Whether you're stopping by for a morning coffee and croissant or picking up a custom cake for a special occasion, Cloudcakes Bakery promises a warm welcome and a delicious experience.

About us

Cloudcakes Bakery was founded in 2025 by a passionate baker named Alvita Oktaviani. With a dream to create a cozy haven for pastry lovers, Alvita combined her love for baking with her vision of a community-centric bakery. Cloudcakes quickly gained popularity for its unique flavors and commitment to quality, becoming a staple in the local food scene. The bakery's mission is to bring joy through delicious baked goods while fostering a sense of community among its patrons.

Our Vision & Mission

At Cloudcakes Bakery, our vision is to be the leading bakery in the community, known for our exceptional quality, innovative flavors, and commitment to sustainability. We aim to create a welcoming space where people can come together to enjoy delicious baked goods made with love and care. Our mission is to consistently deliver high-quality products that exceed our customers' expectations while fostering a sense of community and supporting local suppliers. We are dedicated to sustainability by minimizing waste, using eco-friendly packaging, and sourcing ingredients responsibly. Through our passion for baking and community engagement, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of our customers and the environment.

Our Product

Cloudcakes Bakery offers a diverse range of products, including artisanal breads, pastries, cakes, and cookies. Some of their signature items include the Cloud Nine Croissant, a flaky pastry filled with almond cream; the Velvet Dream Cake, a rich red velvet cake topped with cream cheese frosting; and the Morning Glory Muffin, packed with fruits and nuts for a wholesome start to the day. The bakery also caters to special dietary needs, offering gluten-free and vegan options to ensureveryone can enjoy their delicious treats.

Custom Orders

Cloudcakes Bakery takes pride in offering custom orders for special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, and corporate events. Customers can work closely with the bakery's skilled bakers to design personalized cakes and desserts that reflect their unique tastes and themes. From intricate cake designs to tailored flavor combinations, Cloudcakes ensures that every custom order is crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence. The bakery's dedication to customer satisfaction means that each custom creation is not only visually stunning but also delicious, making any celebration truly memorable.
